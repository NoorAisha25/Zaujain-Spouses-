Our Story

Our journey began
with sincere intentions and the guidance of
Allah (SWT). Through the blessings of
family and the beauty of halal connection,
we came to know one another in a way
that honored modesty, trust, and faith.
With hearts aligned in deen and du'a, we
chose to walk this path together—seeking
tranquility, mercy, and love as Allah
describes in the Qur’an (30:21). As we unite
in the sacred bond of nikah, we humbly
invite you to witness the beginning of our
life together, rooted in faith and filled
with hope for His barakah.
